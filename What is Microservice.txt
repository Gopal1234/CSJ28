What is Microservice?

Microservice is collection of small independently
deployable services work together and modeled around a business domain

All services can be different version

It avoid distributed monolith architecture

It increases productivity
independently all services can be well tested and scalled

enable security concern segregation


Architecting microservices

i.Analyze domain
ii.define bounded context
iii.define entities,aggregates and service
iv.identify service

client-->API gateway--servie1, service2, service3 ...that means all services will be registerd with one central server to lookup and monitor
